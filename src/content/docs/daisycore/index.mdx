## What DaisyCore handles

<div class="feature-matrix">
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Commands</strong><p>Auto-loaded command groups, clean executor helpers, and startup-time availability control.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Menus</strong><p>Inline-first menu flows, viewer-aware items, and lightweight click handling.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Sidebars</strong><p>Keyed lines and stable session ownership for scoreboard-style UI.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Tablists</strong><p>Header/footer rendering with explicit lifecycle cleanup.</p></div>
</div>
