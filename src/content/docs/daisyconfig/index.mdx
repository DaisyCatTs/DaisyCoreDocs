## What DaisyConfig handles

<div class="feature-matrix">
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Codec-driven config</strong><p>Map YAML into real objects with explicit shape and path-aware validation.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Reload handles</strong><p>Control runtime config replacement without throwing away the last working state.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Value decoding</strong><p>Use DaisySeries-backed codecs for materials, sounds, flags, enchantments, and effects.</p></div>
  <div class="feature-card"><strong>Text storage</strong><p>Store shared text here while leaving rendering, placeholders, and viewer awareness in DaisyCore.</p></div>
</div>
